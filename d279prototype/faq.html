Frequently asked questions

Information about our services and more.

How does a virtual consultation work for my pet?

Our virtual consultations are simple and convenient. After scheduling an appointment, you'll receive a secure link to join the video call. During the session, our experienced professionals will discuss your pet's health or behavior concerns, provide advice, and answer any questions you may have.

Schedule your appointment now!

Are virtual consultations suitable for emergency situations?

While virtual consultations are a valuable resource for many pet care needs, they are not intended for emergencies. In urgent situations, please contact your local emergency veterinary clinic or animal hospital for immediate assistance.

What technology do I need for a virtual consultation?

All you need is a computer, tablet, or smartphone with a stable internet connection and a built-in camera. Our platform is user-friendly, and we'll guide you through the process to ensure a seamless virtual experience.

Is my pet's information kept confidential during a virtual consultation?

Absolutely. We prioritize the privacy and security of your pet's information. Our platform complies with all relevant privacy laws, and our team follows strict confidentiality protocols. Rest assured that your pet's details are handled with the utmost care and professionalism.

What types of pet issues can be addressed in a virtual consultation?

Our virtual consultations cover a wide range of pet-related concerns, including medical issues, behavior problems, nutritional queries, and general wellness advice. Whether you're dealing with a specific health condition, seeking training tips, or looking to optimize your pet's nutrition, our experts are here to assist you virtually.

Can I get a prescription for my pet through a virtual consultation?

In some cases, our veterinarians may prescribe medication during a virtual consultation, depending on the nature of your pet's condition. However, certain regulations may require an in-person visit for specific prescriptions. Our team will guide you accordingly, and if necessary, recommend a follow-up with your local veterinary clinic.
