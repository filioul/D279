Our staff

Meet the pet professionals!

Dr. Sarah Thompson

Chief Veterinarian

Dr. Sarah Thompson leads our team with a wealth of experience in veterinary medicine. A graduate of the prestigious School of Veterinary Medicine, she specializes in preventive care and has a keen interest in promoting holistic pet wellness. Outside the clinic, you can find Dr. Thompson exploring hiking trails with her rescue dog, Max, and experimenting with new pet-friendly recipes in her kitchen.

Emily Rodriguez

Certified Pet Behaviorist

Emily is our go-to expert for understanding and addressing your pet's behavior. With a master's degree in animal behavior, she has an exceptional ability to decode and modify challenging behaviors in pets. Beyond her passion for helping animals, Emily enjoys volunteering at local shelters and spending weekends camping with her two adventurous cats, Whiskers and Luna.

Adnan Muhammed

Senior Pet Nutritionist

Adnan is our dedicated pet nutritionist, armed with a degree in Animal Science and a specialization in pet nutrition. His expertise lies in formulating customized diets to meet the unique dietary needs of each pet. When not crafting nutrition plans, Adnan enjoys tending to his urban garden and teaching his mischievous parrot, Mango, new tricks.

Dr. Olivia Chen

Telehealth Specialist

Dr. Chen is at the forefront of our telehealth services, bringing her knowledge and experience in leveraging technology for pet care. With a background in telemedicine and a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine degree, she ensures a seamless and effective virtual consultation experience. Outside of work, Dr. Chen is an avid traveler who loves exploring new cultures and cuisines, always accompanied by her loyal canine companion, Marshmallow.

Alexis Harper

Certified Canine/Feline Behaviorist

Alexis is our skilled canine trainer with a deep understanding of dog behavior and training techniques. Holding certifications in canine training and behavior modification, Alexis excels in helping pet owners build strong, positive relationships with their furry companions. In her free time, she volunteers at a local animal rescue, offering her expertise to rehabilitate and train dogs for adoption. Alexis is also an agility enthusiast, often participating in dog sports competitions with her dog, Jasper.
